Universities Chapter

The USICC Universities Chapter comprises a distinguished network of higher education institutions across both countries committed to advancing US-India academic cooperation. Member universities collaborate on joint degree programs, faculty exchanges, research partnerships, and student mobility initiatives.

The chapter facilitates MOUs, joint research grants, and collaborative curriculum development — building the institutional architecture for lasting academic bonds between the two nations.
